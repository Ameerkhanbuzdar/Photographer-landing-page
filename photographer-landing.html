The Artist

I don't just take
photos. I capture
moments that last.

Born and raised in Karachi, I discovered photography at 16 through my father's vintage Nikon. What started as a hobby became a lifelong obsession with light, emotion, and the quiet poetry of the human face.

I've shot for publications across South Asia and the Middle East, worked with brands from Nike to local artisans, and documented weddings from Lahore to London. Every project, big or small, gets the same dedication.
